Frequently Asked Questions

Some common questions about our TDRAVA roleplay circle

Can I join even if my muse isn’t Chinese?

We mainly focus on Chinese-themed muses, but we’re open to discussing other options as long as it fits with the overall vibe of the circle. Just let us know!

Is it okay to have multiple characters (mulchar) here?

Yes, you’re welcome to have more than one character! Just make sure all your characters stay active and fit in with our China-themed setting.

Are there any rules I should follow when chatting here?

We value friendly and respectful interactions. Avoid sensitive topics, and try to keep conversations related to our Chinese roleplay theme. Have fun but be considerate!

Can we use languages other than Mandarin?

While Mandarin is encouraged to keep the theme authentic, feel free to use other languages when needed. Just try to maintain the Chinese atmosphere as much as possible.

How do I join this roleplay circle?

You can reach out to one of the admins or follow the joining instructions posted in our group. Make sure to read and understand the rules before jumping in!
